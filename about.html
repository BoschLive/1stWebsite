About Us

History

Greendale Community College, founded in 1974 by Russell Borchet, got it's start as a Techincal Computer Science Institution then known as "Greendale Computery College". While we now offer a wide variety of courses across our many departments, Greendale is still commited to growing and imporving our Computer Science Department.

Mission

Greendale Community College makes available collegiate level education to adults of all ages, races, and creeds. Greendale prides itself in 5 categories which the school excels at:

  • Accesability
  • Affordability
  • Air Conditioning
  • Awesome New Friends
  • A lot of classes

Tuition

Greendale introduced the "No More Than 64" promise, which means that students will never pay more than $64 per credit hour.
